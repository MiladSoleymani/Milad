About me

Hello! I'm Milad, a passionate Master's student in Computer Science with a specialization in Artificial Intelligence at Azad University. My academic journey is driven by a profound fascination with how intelligent systems can transform our world.

As a scientist, I’m broadly interested in all aspects of machine learning, and particularly in probabilistic modeling, deep learning, robust and scalable data-driven systems, and applications to life and engineering sciences.

My current research focuses on parameter efficient fine-tuning for language and vision models and the design and development of Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) applications. Through this work, I aim to explore efficient ways to adapt powerful AI models to specific tasks and to build intelligent systems that can leverage external knowledge for more accurate and comprehensive responses.

Looking ahead, I aspire to pursue a Ph.D. in AI research, focusing on designing more robust, accurate, and smaller AI models, alongside exploring Physics-Informed AI. This involves delving into novel architectural designs, advanced optimization techniques, and innovative data-centric approaches to ensure AI systems are not only powerful but also practical and deployable across diverse computational environments. My goal is to contribute to the foundational understanding and development of AI that can generalize effectively, and operate efficiently on resource-constrained platforms.

Outside of my studies, I enjoy contributing to open-source AI projects, working out, and watching movies.
