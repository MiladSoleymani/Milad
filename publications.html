Research Papers

  1. POC-CSP: Parameterised and Orthogonally-Constrained Neural Network for Learning Common Spatial Patterns in EEG

    2024

    Milad Soleymani, Mohammad Ali Ahmadi, Mehdi Ezoji
    Submitted to Neural Networks Journal

    Abstract: We propose POC-CSP, a novel neural network layer that learns Common Spatial Patterns (CSP) with enhanced orthogonality constraints. This approach improves the discriminative power of spatial filters for EEG-based brain-computer interfaces (BCIs) by incorporating parameterized constraints directly into the neural architecture. Our method achieves state-of-the-art performance on multiple BCI datasets while maintaining interpretability of learned spatial patterns.

  2. A Self-supervised Task-agnostic Embedding for Multi-task EEG Classification

    2024

    Milad Soleymani, Hamed Azami, Ali Motie Nasrabadi
    Under Review

    Abstract: This paper presents a self-supervised learning framework for creating universal EEG representations that generalize across different BCI tasks. By leveraging contrastive learning and temporal consistency, our approach learns task-agnostic features from raw EEG signals without requiring labeled data. The learned embeddings significantly improve downstream task performance across motor imagery, emotion recognition, and cognitive load assessment, demonstrating the potential for transfer learning in BCI applications.

  3. A Deep Learning Algorithm for Classifying Grasp Motions using Multi-session EEG Recordings

    2021

    Mohammad Ali Ahmadi, Milad Soleymani, Mehdi Ezoji
    IEEE Access

    Abstract: We present a novel deep learning architecture for classifying grasp motions from EEG signals recorded across multiple sessions. Our approach addresses the challenge of inter-session variability through domain adaptation techniques and temporal convolutional networks. The proposed method achieves 87.3% accuracy on a multi-session dataset, outperforming traditional machine learning approaches by a significant margin.
